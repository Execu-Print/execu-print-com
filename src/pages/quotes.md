**To allow us to quote your project more accurately, please provide the following information about your project:**

**As with all custom jobs, the more you do the less per piece the cost.**
**When we quote pricing for jobs we like to give quantity options so you can see the potential savings of different quantities.**

**Please give us a maximum and minimum quantity.**

## Sign & Graphic Projects

- File type (Illustrator – .AI, .EPS, .PDF – with all fonts outlined and colors identified if required)
- What is the application?
- Size of your project
- Number of colors desired (full color is available)
- Quantity
- Type of material needed, if you have a preference – if not we can suggest the best material usage for your project.
- Indoor or Outdoor (If outdoor, how long do you need it to last?)
- Do you need this laminated.

## Banner Project

- File type (Illustrator – .AI, .EPS, .PDF – with all fonts outlined and colors identified if required)
- Size
- Quantity
- 1-sided or 2-sided
- Indoor or Outdoor use
- Finishing options (tape hem, pole pocket, reinforced hem, extended ropes, etc.)

## Vehicle Graphics, Fleet Wraps & Color Change Wraps

- Because of the custom nature of vehicle graphics, fleet and color change wraps, it is best to set up an appointment for us to go over the vehicle with you and learn more about what you are looking for prior to quoting.
<p class="color-red">
**WE CANNOT GIVE QUOTES OR ESTIMATES OVER THE PHONE OR EMAIL ON VEHICLE GRAPHICS. VEHICLES MUST BE SEEN IN PERSON TO GO OVER MEASUREMENTS, MATERIAL, AND LABOR TO GIVE EVEN A BALLPARK ESTIMATE COST.**
</p>

## Embroidery & Screen Printing

- What kind of application (Shirts, towels, Bags, etc…)
- For garments do you have a preference 100% cotton or 50/50
- Are you supplying the garments
- How many pieces total as well as size breakdown
- How many colors
- How many positions (front, back, sleeves, etc..)

All shirt samples ordered must be paid for.

Absolutely no returns.

All sales final.

## Printing & Copy Projects

- File type (Illustrator – .AI, .EPS, .PDF)
- Size of your project (11×17, 8.5×11, Business Card, etc.)
- Full color or Black and White
- Paper stock (Gloss, Flat, Card Stock, Text Weight)
- Number of pages
- Number of sides
- Artwork [Bleed](/artwork-guidelines-requirements) (Yes or No)
- Quantity
- Bindery/Finishing needs (Fold, Staple, Other)
